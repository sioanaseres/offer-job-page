Mission of the Job

The Web Developer will be responsible for supporting the existing development team in maintaining, building, and testing a wide range of web applications and responsive websites

Responsibilities

  • Maintaining existing sites content and developing new layouts and features based on specifications
  • Responsible for developing responsive websites across multiple devices
  • Technical Deployment to hosting environment
  • Develop HTML based e-newsletters for online marketing campaigns
  • Perform quality assurance on code, both client and server side. Log & monitor any bugs and effectively get these issues resolved
  • Ability to work with and customize WordPress
  • Clients and product database management and support
  • Communication with internal clients to understand their expectations and requirements.
  • Work with team members to determine creative solutions that best meet projects requirements and goals

Skills and Experience Required

  • At least 1 year of working experience in a related field is required for this position but fresh graduates/entry-level applicants with exceptional skills are encouraged to apply
  • Bachelor's degree in computer science, information technology or related field is preferred.
  • Working knowledge of HTML and CSS. Experience with Bootstrap
  • Experience with JavaScript
  • Experience with PHP and MySQL. Understanding of OOP.
  • Experience with WordPress
  • Basic understanding of CMS and Frameworks
  • Basic understanding and application of Linux command-line
  • Strong command of the English Language
  • Basic ability to interpret and analyse data

Key Competencies and Soft Skills

  • A proactive attitude to problem solving and an understanding of the role of development in a creative environment. A passion to learn and grow and the desire to go the extra mile
  • Motivated, driven team player who interacts well with others, and has strong verbal and communication skills. You must be able to express your ideas clearly to others, to communicate technical results to the team and to consult and advise other departments members on project-related issues
  • Ability to demonstrate good time management skills, prioritize projects and perform effectively to tight deadlines
  • Strong attention to detail with the ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously. As a small company, we often have multiple projects running at any one time, you'll be expected to meet multiple deadlines across multiple projects.
  • Self-motivated to learn from others and contribute positively
  • Strong analytical skills

Bonus Skills we'd love to see

  • Familiarity with email marketing software projects
  • Experience with SCRUM/Agile methodology
  • Experience with GIT
  • Experience with Google Analytics
  • Knowledge and/or experience with web services technologies including REST, APIs, JSON etc.
  • Some Photoshop skills
